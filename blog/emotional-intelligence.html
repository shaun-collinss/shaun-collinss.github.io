Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to be able to understand and discern your own emotions. Being able to do this also allows the person the ability to read, percieve and understand the emotion of other people around them. A qoute that really speaks well to this is if you want to go fast go alone, if you want to go far go together.

How is it different to IQ?

Emotional intelligence is about being able to read and percieve your own and others emotions. IQ or Intelligence Quotient is based on peoples own ability to apply their own skills such as word comprehension, math skills, abstract thinking and logical reasoning.

Why is it important?

It's important because it allows for a more inclusive and cohesive working environment. It helps to foster better relationships with those around you and thus be able to help improve everyones experiences to create a work environment for growth.
